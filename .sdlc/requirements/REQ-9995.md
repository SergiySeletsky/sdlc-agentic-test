# Acceptance Criteria for Multi-Distributor Inventory Lookup and Substitution

## Statement

Given a requested part is out of stock at one distributor, When the system performs inventory lookup, Then the system SHALL find equivalent parts from other distributors and substitute them automatically with a substitution accuracy rate of at least 99%.

## Rationale

To ensure ghost inventory is prevented and orders are fulfilled without delay.

# Multi-Distributor Inventory Lookup and Dynamic Substitution

## Statement

The system SHALL perform real-time inventory lookups across multiple distributors and SHALL dynamically substitute equivalent parts automatically when the originally selected parts are out of stock.

## Rationale

Prevent ghost inventory issues and ensure availability of components for configuration fulfillment.

# The system SHALL verify thermal, power logic, and physical constraints to ensure compatibility of all components in the BOM

## Statement

The system SHALL verify thermal, power logic, and physical constraints to ensure compatibility of all components in the Bill of Materials (BOM).

## Rationale

To prevent incompatible configurations and ensure reliable server/rack setups.

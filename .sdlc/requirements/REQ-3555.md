# Multi-Distributor Inventory Lookup and Dynamic Part Substitution - Acceptance Criteria

## Statement

The system SHALL perform real-time inventory lookups across multiple distributors and dynamically substitute equivalent parts when the requested parts are unavailable to prevent ghost inventory.

## Rationale

Ensure availability of parts and prevent order delays due to stockouts.

# Verify Thermal, Power, and Physical Compatibility of BOM Components

## Statement

The system SHALL verify thermal, power logic, and physical constraints for all components included in the Bill of Materials to ensure compatibility.

## Rationale

To prevent incompatible component selections and ensure reliable server/rack configurations.

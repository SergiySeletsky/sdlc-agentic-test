# Acceptance Criteria for Thermal and Power Compatibility Checks

## Statement

The system SHALL accurately calculate total thermal and power requirements and SHALL flag any incompatibility or violation of physical constraints within the configuration.

## Rationale

To prevent deployment of incompatible or unsafe hardware configurations.

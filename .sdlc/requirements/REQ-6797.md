# Acceptance Criteria for Multi-Distributor Inventory Lookup and Dynamic Substitution

## Statement

The system SHALL successfully query multiple distributors in real-time and SHALL automatically substitute equivalent parts when original parts are out of stock.

## Rationale

To maintain configuration availability and prevent order delays due to inventory issues.

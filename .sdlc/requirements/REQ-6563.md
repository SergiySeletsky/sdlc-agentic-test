# The system SHALL perform real-time multi-distributor inventory lookup and dynamically substitute equivalent parts when out of stock

## Statement

The system SHALL perform real-time inventory lookup across multiple distributors and dynamically substitute equivalent parts automatically when the requested parts are out of stock.

## Rationale

To prevent ghost inventory and ensure order fulfillment without delay.

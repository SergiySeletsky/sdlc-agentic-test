# Multi-Distributor Real-Time Inventory Lookup and Dynamic Equivalent Part Substitution

## Statement

The system SHALL perform real-time inventory lookup across multiple distributors and SHALL dynamically substitute equivalent parts when original parts are out of stock.

## Rationale

To prevent ghost inventory and ensure availability of all components in the BOM by leveraging multi-distributor data and dynamic substitution.

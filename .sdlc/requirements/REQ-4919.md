# Acceptance Criteria for Natural Language to BOM Parsing

## Statement

Given user inputs configuration in natural language, When the system processes the input, Then the system SHALL generate a complete Bill of Materials including chassis, riser cables, rail kits, and PSUs with 100% component coverage and zero parsing errors.

## Rationale

To ensure natural language parsing produces accurate and complete BOM output.

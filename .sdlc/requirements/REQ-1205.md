# The system SHALL parse natural language input to generate a complete Bill of Materials including chassis, riser cables, rail kits, and PSUs

## Statement

The system SHALL parse user natural language input to generate a complete Bill of Materials (BOM) including chassis, riser cables, rail kits, and PSUs.

## Rationale

To enable users to input configurations in natural language and obtain a structured BOM automatically, reducing manual effort and errors.

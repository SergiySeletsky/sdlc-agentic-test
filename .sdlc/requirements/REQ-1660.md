# Perform Real-Time Multi-Distributor Inventory Lookup and Dynamic Substitution

## Statement

The system SHALL perform real-time inventory lookups across multiple distributors and SHALL dynamically substitute equivalent parts when original parts are out of stock.

## Rationale

To prevent ghost inventory issues and ensure availability of all needed components.

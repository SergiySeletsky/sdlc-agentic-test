# Ghost Inventory Prevention via Multi-Distributor Lookups

## Statement

The system SHALL perform real-time inventory lookups across multiple distributors and dynamically swap equivalent parts when originals are unavailable to prevent out-of-stock scenarios.

## Rationale

To ensure parts availability and prevent delays from ghost inventory situations.
